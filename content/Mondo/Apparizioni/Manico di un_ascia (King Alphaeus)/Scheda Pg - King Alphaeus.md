### **Class Features**

*   **Weapon Aptitude (Ex):** Can adjust weapon-specific feats after 1 hour of practice with a different weapon.
    
*   **Battle Clarity (Ex):** Add INT bonus (+2) to Reflex saves when not flat-footed.
    
*   **Battle Ardor (Ex):** Add INT bonus (+2) to confirm critical hits.
    
*   **Battle Cunning (Ex):** Add INT bonus (+2) to damage rolls against flat-footed or flanked opponents.
    
*   **Uncanny Dodge (Ex):** Cannot be caught flat-footed.
    
*   **Improved Uncanny Dodge (Ex):** Cannot be flanked.
    
*   **Stance Mastery (Ex):** May have two stances active simultaneously (not until level 20, so ignore this for level 8).
    

* * *

### **Feats**

1.  **Power Attack:** Trade attack bonus for damage (–3 attack, +6 damage with two-handed weapon).
    
2.  **Weapon Focus (Greataxe):** +1 bonus on attack rolls with greataxe.
    
3.  **Leap Attack:** Multiply extra damage from Power Attack when making a charge with a jump.
    
4.  **Weapon Specialization (Greataxe):** +2 bonus on damage rolls with greataxe.
    
5.  **Improved Initiative:** +4 bonus on initiative checks.
    

_(Note: Warblades receive a bonus feat at level 5, selected from the list of fighter bonus feats.)_
